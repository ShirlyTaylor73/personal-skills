### #3 Pi06 (Physical Intelligence et al., arXiv 2025)

- 标题：π*0.6: a VLA That Learns From Experience
- 作者：Physical Intelligence team（Ali Amin, Raichelle Aniceto, Ashwin Balakrishna, Kevin Black, Sergey Levine, Chelsea Finn 等数十人）
- 发表年份：2025
- venue：arXiv preprint
- arXiv ID：2511.18674
- 中文摘要：本文研究如何让视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型通过真实世界部署中的强化学习不断进步。作者提出 RECAP（RL with Experience and Corrections via Advantage-conditioned Policies），一种通用方法，通过 advantage 条件化将异质数据（人工示范、自主 rollout、专家干预）整合进自我改进流程。RECAP 先用离线 RL 预训练通用 VLA 得到 π*0.6，再通过下游任务的机器人数据收集进行专项化。π*0.6 基于 π0.6（Gemma 3 4B 主干 + 860M 动作专家），新增对二值化 advantage 指示器的条件化能力。在叠衣、装配纸盒、用专业咖啡机制作浓缩咖啡等真实任务上，RECAP 在最难任务上吞吐量翻倍以上，失败率约减半，并能让机器人连续 13 小时制作咖啡、连续 2 小时折叠从未见过的衣物。
- 核心方法：基于 advantage 条件化的迭代离线 RL，在 flow matching VLA 上端到端训练，结合示范+自主 rollout+人类干预三类数据 〔p.1: "RL with Experience and Corrections via Advantage-conditioned Policies (RECAP)"〕；关键创新：(1) 二值化 advantage 指示器作为 prompt 条件 〔p.2: "adds the ability to condition on binarized advantage values"〕；(2) 多任务 distributional value function 估计步数到成功 〔p.4: "multi-task distributional value function p_phi(V|o_t,ℓ)"〕；(3) Human-gated DAgger 风格干预与自主 rollout 联合训练 〔p.2: "uses both expert interventions and fully autonomous experience"〕
- 主要贡献：提出 RECAP 通用 RL 配方将奖励反馈贯穿预训练到部署后微调，并在长时序灵巧任务上首次显著提升 VLA 吞吐与鲁棒性 〔p.2: "show, for the first time, that a general-purpose reinforcement learning recipe...can significantly improve both the robustness and throughput of VLA models"〕；提出可扩展的 advantage 条件化策略提取方法替代 PPO/AWR 〔p.3: "a simple and scalable advantage-conditioned policy extraction method"〕；π*0.6 在硬任务上吞吐 2× 提升、失败率约减半 〔p.2: "more than doubles the throughput on some of the hardest tasks, and can decrease failure rates by 2× or more"〕
- 与本调研主题的关系：作为 Physical Intelligence π 系列截至 2026-05 的最新版本，是 VLA 通过 RL 自我改进的代表性工作，对应"训练范式·后训练强化学习"维度
- 优点：在 flow matching VLA 上端到端 RL，吞吐和成功率显著提升至 90%+ 实用水平 〔p.9: "the success rate of the final π*0.6 model is in the 90%+ range"〕；可针对特定 failure mode 用少量数据修复（97% 成功率） 〔p.11: "results in a policy that succeeds 97% of the time"〕；支持示范、干预、自主三类异质数据 〔p.5: "data Dπref consists of all of the data collected so far"〕
- 局限性：系统非完全自主，仍依赖人工标注奖励、人工干预与重置 〔p.11: "our system is not fully autonomous: it relies on human labeling and effort for reward feedback, interventions, and episode resets"〕；exploration 较朴素，主要靠策略随机性和人工干预 〔p.11: "exploration is largely greedy, relying on stochasticity in the policy and human interventions"〕；当前是迭代离线更新而非完全在线 RL 〔p.11: "RECAP performs iterated 'offline' updates...rather than running a fully online RL loop"〕
- 典型应用场景：真实家庭中折叠多种衣物、工厂中装配包装纸盒、用专业咖啡机制作多种咖啡饮品 〔p.1: "fold laundry in real homes, reliably assemble boxes, and make espresso drinks using a professional espresso machine"〕
- 数据集：预训练用数万小时多任务多机器人示范数据 〔p.4: "tens of thousands of hours of demonstrations from numerous tasks and a variety of different robots"〕；下游任务在每次迭代收集 300 条（衬衫）或 600 自主+360 干预（盒子）轨迹 〔p.10: "We collect 300 trajectories on four robots in each iteration...600 autonomous trials and 360 trials with interventions"〕
- 评价指标：吞吐量（每小时成功完成任务数）+ 成功率（人工评分聚合的成功比例） 〔p.9: "Throughput measures the number of successful task executions per hour...Success rate measures the proportion of episodes that succeed"〕
- benchmark 数值：
  - Laundry (T-shirt strict, failure removal) / 成功率 / 97% 〔p.11, Fig.12〕
  - Diverse Laundry & Espresso / 吞吐量 / 较 SFT 翻倍以上 〔p.9, Fig.7〕
  - Box assembly (子阶段折盒/贴标) / 成功率 / 约 90% 〔p.10, Fig.8〕
  - Box assembly / 吞吐量 / 第二次迭代后 2× 提升 〔p.10, Fig.9〕
  - 失败率整体降幅 / 失败率 / 约 0.5×（即降低约 2 倍） 〔p.1, 摘要〕
- 一句话评述：π0.5 之后 PI 团队首次将完整离线 RL 配方落到 flow-matching VLA 上端到端训练，是 VLA 走向"实用化部署后持续改进"的里程碑式工作
